ShuttleOFX

Create, share and play online with image processing plugins.

Welcome on board

The ShuttleOFX platform is designed to share OpenFX plugins between plugins creators (industrials, developpers, researchers, students, ...) and graphic artists.

Following the TuttleOFX initiative, the ambition is to promote a standard way to create image processing algorithms usable across softwares.

What is OFX?

OFX is an open API for writing visual effects plug-ins for a wide variety of applications, such as video editing and compositing systems. This guide demonstrates by example the low level C APIs that defines OFX.

Do you write visual effects or image processing software? Do you have an application which deals with moving images and hosts plug-ins or would like to host plug-ins? Then OFX is for you.
